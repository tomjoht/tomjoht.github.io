RJ Jacquez, senior product evangelist at Adobe.com, talks about the new Technical Communication Suite from Adobe, which includes RoboHelp 7, Captivate 3, Acrobat 3D version 8, and Framemaker 8. Topics we discuss include the following:

  • What the Technical Communication Suite includes and how each product interacts
  • Integration of Captivate with RoboHelp
  • Integration of Framemaker with RoboHelp
  • Support for 35 plus languages
  • Ability to embed flash in Acrobat 3D PDFs
  • Experience with the Tech Comm blog
  • Roadmap for the Technical Communication Suite
  • Adobe's emphasis on suites
