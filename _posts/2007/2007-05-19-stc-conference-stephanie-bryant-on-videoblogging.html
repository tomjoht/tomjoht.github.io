Stephanie talks about videoblogging as a tool for promoting yourself or for communicating with others. She explains the equipment she uses and the process for producing videoblogs. Her book, Videoblogging for Dummies, is available from her website: www.mortaine.com. Stephanie also discusses interesting ways lawyers can use videoblogging to educate their clients.
