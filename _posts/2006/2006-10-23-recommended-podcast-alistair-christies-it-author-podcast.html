Download MP3 (from Alistair's site)
Duration: 30 min.

If you like the Tech Writer Voices podcast, then you'll probably also like Alistair Christie's podcast, which is called IT Author Podcast and can be found here: http://www.itauthor.com/wordpress/category/podcasts. Alistair is a technical writer from the UK, and has a pleasing conversational style to his podcasts. He also has an incredible depth of information about technical writing. I was honored to be a guest on his recent show.
