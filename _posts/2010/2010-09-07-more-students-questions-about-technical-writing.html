In this podcast (another monologue), I respond to more student questions about technical writing. The questions are as follows:

  1. Question 1 - Who/what are the people and purposes that you write for now? And in the past?
  2. Question 2 - What are the kinds of things that you write?
  3. Question 3 - What are the processes, methods, practices you use to write those things?
  4. Question 4 - What are the technologies you use and how do you use them?
